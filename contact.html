Important Information

  • In case of immediate danger, always call 100 (Police Emergency) first.
  • Document any incidents of discrimination with photos, videos, or written records.
  • Seek legal advice before taking formal action.
  • Many of these services provide free legal aid to those who cannot afford it.
  • Contact details may vary by state/region - verify with local authorities.
